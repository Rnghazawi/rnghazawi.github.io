Biography

Rayed Ghazawi is a PhD candidate at the University of Bristol, specializing in Natural Language Processing, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning. His doctoral research focuses on the development of intelligent assessment systems for Arabic essays, integrating active learning strategies and large language models.

Before beginning his PhD, Rayed served as the Head of Registration and Scheduling at the Deanship of Admission and Registration, Umm Al-Qura University, where he now lectures in the Computing College. He actively publishes in international conferences and is passionate about bridging education and AI to improve Arabic language technologies.
